Primary Logo

Navy + Gold Mark

The primary logo is a stylized spire inspired by St. Peter's Church in Riga, Latvia, enclosed in a navy circle with a gold beacon dot at the peak. The stepped silhouette evokes both architectural heritage and upward market momentum.

Social Media

Profile Avatars

Use the icon-only mark for social avatars (X, LinkedIn, Discord). The circle shape works natively in circular crops. The dark background behind the circle provides safe padding.

Usage Guidelines

Do's and Don'ts

DO
Use the logo at its original proportions with adequate clear space.
DON'T
Don't stretch, squish, or distort the proportions.
DO
Use brighter gold (#fbbf24) on very dark backgrounds for contrast.
DON'T
Don't place on backgrounds that clash with the gold beacon.
DO
RigaCap
Keep consistent spacing between icon and text.
DON'T
RIGACAP
Don't alter the text styling, capitalization, or typeface.
