Three-Tier Symbol Extraction

When a monitored account tweets, the system extracts stock mentions using three layers of progressively looser matching:

Tier 1: Cashtags ($NVDA)

Always trusted. Unambiguous stock mention. Highest confidence match.

Tier 2: Bare Tickers (MSFT)

Only matched when financial context is detected in the surrounding text. Prevents false positives.

Tier 3: Company Names

Word-boundary match ("nvidia" → NVDA). Most aggressive, but catches casual mentions that cashtags miss.

Trade Matching

Matched symbols checked against real model portfolio exits (primary) and walk-forward sim trades (fallback). Min 5% return. Best match selected.

Example Flow

@TastyTrade posts: “NVDA breaking out of consolidation, watching for entry”

RigaCap reply (auto-generated): “Our ensemble flagged NVDA on Jan 15 when all three factors aligned. It's up 12% since. Full breakdown: rigacap.com/track-record”

Admin receives one-click approve/skip email. Approved replies publish instantly via Twitter API v2.

Instagram Comment Automation

The engine also monitors comments on our own Instagram posts and generates warm, on-brand replies via Claude. Not salesy — genuine engagement that builds community. Comments under 10 characters are filtered, dedup prevents double-replies, and all responses go through admin approval.

Spam Prevention & Brand Safety

  • 7-day dedup: Never reply twice to the same account/symbol combination within a week
  • Prompt guardrails: "Never give financial advice," "Never start with 'Great post!'," "No hashtags in replies"
  • Admin approval required: Every reply goes through human review before publishing
  • Rate limiting: Max reply scan 4x daily to avoid API limits and appearing bot-like

Auto-Publish

Full Audit Trail
