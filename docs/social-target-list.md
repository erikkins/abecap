## Engagement Playbook

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- Follow all accounts across X and Instagram
- Like 3-5 posts per day across the list
- No promotional content — just genuine engagement

### Week 3-4: Reply & Add Value
- Reply to 5-10 posts per day with substantive, data-driven comments
- Share walk-forward performance data when relevant (never unsolicited)
- Start "We Called It" posts referencing real trades

### Month 2: Quote Tweets & Thought Leadership
- Quote-tweet with supporting or complementary analysis
- Tag relevant accounts when sharing market regime analysis
- DM smaller accounts (<50K followers) to introduce RigaCap

### Month 3+: Partnership Outreach
- Reach out to @AlpacaHQ, @TradersPost, @TradeZella for integration partnerships
- Pitch story angles to @KateRooney and other fintech journalists
- Cross-promote with complementary tools (@TrendSpider, @LuxAlgo, @Quantpedia)

### Priority Targets (highest reciprocity)
Focus engagement here first — mid-tier accounts most likely to engage back:
- @thetraderisk, @mikeharrisNY, @QuantStart, @Quantpedia, @TradersPost, @AlpacaHQ
