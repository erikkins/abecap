RigaCap is a **signal service**. Editorial-financial-publication framing (FT, Economist, Stratechery, Grant's) is the **aesthetic register** only — typography, voice, prose discipline, restraint. It is NOT a product category claim.

**Why:** Apr 30 2026, when drafting social bios, the marketing strategy doc had me lead with "publication" in customer copy. Erik pushed back: "we're a signal product first, with an Economist or FT vibe." Calling ourselves a publication makes prospects expect to pay for the writing, when they're actually paying for the decisions. The doc was amended (§5) to make the distinction explicit.

**How to apply:**
- Bios, taglines, lead copy: frame as signal service. "Equity signals, walk-forward validated." "An equity signal service for ___." "Disciplined equity signals."
- Voice and aesthetic: keep the FT/Economist register fully — restraint, em-dashes, no hype, considered prose, specific numbers.
- Newsletter is genuinely a publication channel within the product. That's contextually fine; just don't extend the label to the whole brand.
- The marketing strategy doc §5 now codifies this; defer to it if the question comes up again.
